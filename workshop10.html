WORKSHOP ON
LASER-INDUCED
INCANDESCENCE
June 24-27, 2024 | MUSKOKA, CANADA
Abstracts due May 3, 2024.
Sponsored by:

This meeting brings together researchers from particle, combustion, and atmospheric sciences to exchange and discuss recent trends and applications of laser-induced incandescence for characterizing aerosols – including soot and engineered nanoparticles – as well as complementary techniques for particle characterization.

Throughout the workshop, a single stream of oral presentations on emerging research will be held daily. Poster sessions will also be held daily. Additional discussion sessions on a range of topics will provide a summary of progress in the field.

This is an in-person only event.

For past workshops please visit LIIscience.org.
